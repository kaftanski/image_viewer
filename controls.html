IMI Image Viewer Controls

Mouse buttons

Left click

Place a marker at the current mouse position.

Right click

Click and hold to change window and level of the image. By moving the mouse upwards or downwards, to increase or decrease the level. Moving the mouse right or left widens or shrinks the window.

Middle click

Toggle pan/zoom mode. Left click and drag now pans the image, right click and drag zooms in or out.

Key bindings

x change orientation of the image to the YZ plane (sagittal view)
y change orientation of the image to the XZ plane (coronal view)
z change orientation of the image to the XY plane (axial view)
arrow-up move one slice forward
arrow-down move one slice back
page-up move multiple slices forward
page-down move multiple slices back
